Why Import Cars from the USA?

We compared the US markets with European ones and realized that buying a car in America is several times more profitable, paradoxical as it may sound. This is due to well-thought-out logistics, the level of development of car evaluation services, and the car purchasing process itself.

Most US citizens lease a car for several years, and the leasing company takes care of regular car maintenance throughout its usage period. As a result, cars from the USA are one of the best choices for Ukrainian car enthusiasts.

Why Are Prices So Low?

Damaged cars from the USA are purchased from insurance auctions. At these auctions, a car loses half of its value even due to minimal damage. Considering the costs such as purchase, delivery, customs clearance, and repairs, the price of a similar condition car in Ukraine would be 35-50% higher, while new cars would cost astronomical amounts.
